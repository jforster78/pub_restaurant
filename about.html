About Us

Relax in a comfortable and homely atmosphere, whilst enjoying lunch or dinner chosen from our traditional British pub menu. Our innovative dishes are mouth watering. With carefully selected, high quality, fresh, regional produce, food is available throughout the day from 12.00 noon to 9.30pm and Sundays 12pm to 8pm. Our home cooked dishes are perfectly complimented by our well considered beers and wine list, offering all our visitors plenty of choice.
