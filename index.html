The Farmhouse

At The Farmhouse you will find a warm friendly atmosphere, were friends and family's can enjoy a drink and relax. We offer a traditional Pub menu were you can find classics, such as the Farmhouse Beef Burger with Pulled Pork, our Homemade Steak and Ale Pie and our famous Fish and Chips. But do not stop there have a look at our selection of delicious desserts and coffees. In the winter months the Bar and Restaurant is warmed by log fires, whist taking in the atmosphere.
